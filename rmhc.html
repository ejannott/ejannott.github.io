Project Details

RMHC of Ann Arbor Mobile App Design

This is nonprofit work that I did for the Ronald McDonald Family House here in Ann Arbor. They were redesigning their website to meet the new design standards set by the national organization, and in the process decided they wanted a mobile app as well. I made a high fidelity prototype for the backend programmers to use as a template in their creation of the app.

Before this app, families would come into the RMHC Family House in Ann Arbor and be given a large binder that had a number of documents with information about that house. Many of these documents were outdated, redundant, and hard to locate. The mobile app remedied the need for house guests to easily find the information their need in a convenient location.
